Any Questions?

Find answers to the most common questions our students ask. If you can't find your answer here, feel free to contact us.

What programming languages are used in the courses?

Our Data Structures & Algorithms course is taught primarily in Python and JavaScript, two of the most in-demand languages for technical interviews. The Generative AI course uses Python, which is the standard for machine learning and AI development.

Are there any prerequisites for the courses?

For the DSA Mastery course, a basic understanding of any programming language is helpful. For the Generative AI & LLMs course, you should be comfortable with Python and have a foundational knowledge of programming concepts. We cover all the advanced topics from the ground up.

Will I get a certificate upon completion?

Yes, upon successful completion of all modules and projects within a course, you will receive a verifiable certificate that you can share on your LinkedIn profile and resume to showcase your new skills to potential employers.

Do you offer career support after the course?

Absolutely. Our career-focused approach includes resume reviews, mock interview sessions, and access to our exclusive job board. Our goal is not just to teach you but to help you succeed in your career.
